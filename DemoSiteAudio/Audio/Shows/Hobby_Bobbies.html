Hobby Bobbies

Radio 4 sitcom about community policing. Written by Dave Lamb and starring Richie Webb and Nick Walker. Stars Richie Webb, Nick Walker, Sinead Keenan, Chris Emmett, Noddy Holder and Leon Herbert

Series 1



Episodes


Dangerous Minds

Geoff's decision to finally rebel against his domineering Dad doesn't go quite as he'd have liked.

Dangerous Driving

Our heroes decide to act on dangerous driving in the town, starting with colleague Jermain.

Dangerous Posters

The useless officers' crackdown on fly-posting leads to the disappearance of 'Daddy Warbucks' and the sight of Bernie in a bright red wig.

Dangerous Dogs

Our heroes are dispatched to investigate a nuisance caller and her dangerous dog -but stumble into the local drugs racket.

Series 2



Episodes


Sinkhole

When an enormous sinkhole opens up, the police need to do a little more than 'look into it'.

Vice

The tiny Haling police force is thrown into confusion when the Guv orders a crackdown on vice in their patch. Can social media help unveil the mysterious Big Brenda ?

Blackmail

A little bit of murky police history comes back to haunt the Haling squad.Will the stench of corruption overpower the whiff of lager at the inter - force darts tournament?

Burglaries

Nigel takes up free running in the hope that physical fitness can improve his performance at work.But what, if anything, will identify the mysterious hand cream thief ?
